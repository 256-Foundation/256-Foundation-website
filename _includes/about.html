Our Mission

To educate individuals, organizations, and businesses about Bitcoin mining and energy through Free and Open Source development (FOSS). We support educators and developers with grants while they work to advance Bitcoin mining and energy initiatives; at our mining lab we equip developers with resources for continued success. We believe in FOSS development and we pledge that every project from this foundation will always be made available through FOSS and public domain contributions.

This foundation was formed in February 2024 by Rod @bitkite and @econoalchemist.
